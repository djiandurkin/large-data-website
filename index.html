Welcome to the House Music Database

Here at HMDB, we strive to be housekeepers. The ones who maintain a recorded history of the music that we cherish, and that has spawned a worldwide love for all things house. Not everyone understands house music. It's a spiritual thing. A body thing. A soul thing.
